Why Word Journal?

Works everywhere

Select text in Safari, PDFs, Notes, Slack — any app. Shift+Click or double-tap Option to see the definition instantly.

Stay in flow

No switching apps or opening tabs. A floating popup appears right next to the word. One click to add definitions to your journal.

Build your vocabulary

Save definitions as you read. Search, sort, hear pronunciation, and export to CSV for flashcards or study tools.

See it in action

Get started

Download the app, grant accessibility permission when prompted, then select any word and Shift+Click (or double-tap Option) to look it up.
